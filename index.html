    How do SMBs manage inventory and pricing?

    Use the control center to upload purchases, taxes, and supplier costs. Marxia auto-suggests menu pricing to maintain target margins.

    Can I hide delivery charges from consumers?

    Yes. Toggle delivery fee visibility per order or per store. Consumers still receive a transparent invoice that meets fiscal requirements.

    What analytics are available in freemium vs premium?

    Freemium delivers monthly trendlines and anomaly alerts. Premium unlocks day-to-year granularity, predictive waste insights, and AI-crafted recommendations.

    How is security enforced?

    We enforce PCI DSS 4.0 controls, security headers, MFA, encrypted secrets, and integrate with SIEM providers for centralized visibility.
